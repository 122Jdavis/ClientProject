Instruments in the Band

Trumpet

Brass instrument that has a distinct sound, and is known for being the lead melody in Jazz.

Trombone

Brass instrument. Has a slider that lowers the pitch of the note played, changing how it sounds

Clarinet

Squidward's instrument in Spongebob. A woodwind instrument that can play notes at high and low pitches

Double Bass

Big string instrument that's used to support jazz songs with low pitched sounds

Drums

Loud, cool, and one of the most popular instruments. Can usually be heard throughout for entire songs.
